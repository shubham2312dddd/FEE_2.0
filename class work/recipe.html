Your Paneer Kohlapuri Recipe

a typical maharastrian taste profile,with very rich,complex spice notes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Coconut:1 cup(70 gms approx.),grated
  • Dry Red:Chillies:4 pieces
  • Onion:2,finely choppes
  • Paneer:750 gms,cut into cubes
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together in a bowl.

    5 minutes

  2. Whisk wet ingredients in a separate bowl.

    3 minutes

  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, and mix until smooth.

    2 minutes

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

    1 minute

  5. Pour batter onto skillet and cook until bubbles form on the surface.

    2 minutes

  6. Flip and cook until golden brown.

    2 minutes
